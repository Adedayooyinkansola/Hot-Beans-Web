Join Our Team

At Hot Beans Web, we believe that great websites start with great people. We're always on the lookout for passionate, creative, and ambitious individuals to join our growing team. If you're ready to bring fresh ideas, build amazing digital experiences, and grow your career with a company that values innovation, we want to hear from you!

Here are some of the job vacancies available at Hot Beans Web:

We are seeking a full stack web developer with strong skills in both front-end and back-end technologies. You will work on a wide range of projects, from building dynamic websites to creating APIs and database-driven applications. You'll collaborate closely with designers and other developers to deliver modern, scalable web solutions. We are looking for someone who is passionate about clean code, problem solving, and continuous learning.

Specifications:

  • Degree in Computer Science or 2+ years commercial experience
  • Proficient in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Node.js, and React
  • Solid understanding of databases (SQL and MongoDB)
  • Familiar with Git, REST APIs, and modern development workflows

Required Skills: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, React, MongoDB, SQL

Hot Beans Web is looking for a creative web designer who can craft beautiful and user-friendly website layouts. You will be responsible for turning ideas into visually appealing designs that work seamlessly across all devices. If you are passionate about design, detail-oriented, and up-to-date with the latest design trends, we'd love to hear from you.

Specifications:

  • Qualification in Graphic Design, Web Design or relevant experience
  • Strong skills in Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop
  • Good knowledge of HTML and CSS
  • A strong portfolio demonstrating modern, responsive design
  • Basic knowledge of JavaScript is a plus

Required Skills: Adobe XD, Figma, Photoshop, HTML, CSS, basic JavaScript

We are hiring a back end web developer to help power our web applications. You will work on server-side logic, build robust APIs, manage databases, and ensure our systems are secure and scalable. If you love creating efficient back-end systems and enjoy solving technical challenges, this role is for you.

Specifications:

  • Degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience
  • Proficient with Node.js, PHP, and working with MySQL/MongoDB databases
  • Experience building RESTful APIs
  • Understanding of server security and optimization

Required Skills: PHP, Node.js, MySQL, MongoDB, RESTful APIs

We're looking for a QA Tester who is passionate about delivering high-quality web applications. You will test applications, identify bugs, and ensure everything runs smoothly before release. If you have an eye for detail and enjoy breaking things to make them better, you'll be a perfect fit.

Specifications:

  • Experience in software/web application testing
  • Knowledge of manual testing and automated testing tools (e.g., Selenium)
  • Good problem-solving and communication skills
  • Ability to write clear bug reports and test plans

Required Skills: Manual Testing, Automated Testing Tools (e.g., Selenium), Attention to Detail, Problem-Solving Skills
